facilities

At Hub Hotel we look to integrate coworking, co-living and co-learning experience with the most up-to-date and convenient service: 24-hour reception, express check-out, buffet style breakfast, business center, reading area, beverage bar and much more.

We are proficient in English, Chinese, Japanese and Korean. We have high-speed Wifi throughout the hotel, which makes us perfect for business meetings, team gatherings, company retreats, lectures & talks, local cultural tours, and other group activities.
