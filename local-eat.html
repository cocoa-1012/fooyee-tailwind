Taoyuan’s Zhongli district is a cultural hub of the Hakka, Fukien, Hokkien, Southeast Asian and Taiwanese, its diverse cuisine makes it a foodie’s paradise. Try the famous Taiwanese beef noodle soup, Hakka veggie buns, Burmese or Thai food, the choices are limitless.
